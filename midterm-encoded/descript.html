Data Description

Call Number
Unique Call ID of type integer. There are 10,166 unique calls.
Call Type
Incident call type. Varies between 18 categories.
Call Date
Date of call in MM/DD/YYYY format. Varies between day 1-27 of December.
Dispatch_DtTm
Dispatch date and time for the specific call in MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM:SS format. Varies between day 1-27 of December
dispatch_day
Gives the numerical day value of when the call took place
hour_dispatch
Gives the numerical 24-hour value of when the call took place.
weekday_dispatch
Gives the day of the week when the call took place
weekday_dispatch_num
Gives the day of the week when the call took place in numerical format.
Response_DtTm
Response date and time for the specific call in MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM:SS format. Varies between day 1-27 of December
On_Scene_DtTm
Onscene date and time for the specific call in MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM:SS format. Varies between day 1-27 of December
total_response
Minute difference between Dispatch_DtTm and On_Scene_DtTm. Values vary between 1 and 117 minutes
Call_Final_Disposition
Final conclusion reached for particular call. Varies between 14 categories
City
City in the Bay Area. Varies between 6 cities.
Zipcode_of_Incident
Unique zipcode of where the incident took place. Varies between 27 zipcodes.
Original_Priority
Priority provided by dispatch at the beginning of the call. Varies between 1 - 3.
Priority
Priority provided by dispatch. Varies between 1 - 3.
Final_Priority
Priority provided by dispatch at the end of the call. Varies between 1 - 3.
Call_Type_Group
Category of incident type. Varies between 4 categories.
call_type_group_num
Numerical value of cateogry of incident tpe. Varies between 1-4.
Neighborhood_District
District the incident took place it. Varies between 42 categories.
